Frequently Asked Questions

How long does company formation take?

Formation times vary by country: Portugal (5-10 days), Netherlands (1-2 weeks), Spain (15-20 days), and Slovenia (2 months). These are standard timeframes and can be expedited with premium services.

Can I form a company remotely?

Yes, all four jurisdictions allow remote formation through proper documentation and power of attorney arrangements. Portugal and Slovenia offer fully online processes.

Do I need a local director?

Spain, Portugal, and Netherlands do not require local directors. Slovenia may require a local representative for non-resident directors, which our services can provide.

What are the ongoing compliance requirements?

All countries require annual financial statements, tax returns, and registry updates. Specific requirements vary by jurisdiction and business activity level.
