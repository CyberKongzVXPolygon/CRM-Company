Frequently Asked Questions

Which countries are best for startups?

Low capital requirements (€1), fast formation, and supportive business environments make these ideal for startups.

Where can I get the lowest tax rates?

Lowest corporate tax rates in the EU with attractive incentives for businesses and holding structures.

Which countries offer the best international opportunities?

Extensive treaty networks and strategic locations provide optimal international business opportunities.

What are the fastest formation options?

Fastest formation times with Portugal's "Empresa na Hora" and Netherlands' efficient processes.

Which jurisdictions are best for holding companies?

Netherlands and Slovenia are excellent for holding companies due to their extensive tax treaty networks and participation exemption regimes.

Can I incorporate remotely?

Yes, all four jurisdictions allow remote formation. Portugal and Slovenia offer fully online processes, while Spain and Netherlands can be completed with proper documentation.
