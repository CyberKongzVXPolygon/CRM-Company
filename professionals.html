Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to become a partner?

We welcome professionals with relevant experience in law, accounting, consulting, or financial services. You should have an established practice or business with clients who might benefit from European company formation services.

How and when do I get paid?

Commissions are paid monthly via bank transfer or PayPal. Payments are processed within 30 days of the client's payment confirmation. You can track all earnings in real-time through your partner portal.

Is there a minimum commitment or quota?

No, there are no minimum commitments or quotas. You can refer clients at your own pace. However, inactive partners (no referrals for 12 months) may be subject to account review.

Can I offer services under my own brand?

Yes, we offer white-label solutions for qualified partners. This allows you to offer company formation services under your own brand while we handle the backend processes.

What support do you provide to partners?

Partners receive comprehensive support including marketing materials, training, dedicated account management, priority customer service, and ongoing regulatory updates.

How do I track my referrals and commissions?

Our partner portal provides real-time tracking of all referrals, their status, commission amounts, and payment history. You'll also receive detailed monthly reports.
