Spain S.L. Requirements

Minimum Capital

€3,006 minimum share capital required, with at least 25% paid up at incorporation.

Shareholders

1 minimum shareholder required. Can be individuals or legal entities of any nationality.

Directors

1 minimum director required. No residency requirements for directors.

Registered Office

Spanish address required for registered office. Can be commercial or residential.

Company Name

Unique name ending with "S.L." or "Sociedad Limitada" must be reserved.

Corporate Purpose

Defined activities must be specified in the Articles of Association.
